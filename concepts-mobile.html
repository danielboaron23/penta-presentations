Editorial Noir

Which one, when

Pick by the job, not the looks.

Ship the app profile
→ Classic

Lowest risk, fully on-system, desktop parity. The safe default that converts and reassures on the phone.

Make people stop
→ Immersive

For a hero seller or a "featured creator" spotlight — the scroll-dive earns attention right in the feed.

Sell a premium brand
→ Editorial Noir

For top-tier creative sellers where the page itself is part of the portfolio. A distinct, ownable identity.
