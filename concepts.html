One by one
01

Classic

Penta design system · profile.html

The dependable marketplace seller page — instantly familiar, fast, and built to convert. Reuses ~70% of the existing Penta components, so it ships with the lowest risk.

Interactive bento gallery — drag to reorder, click → modal with a draggable thumbnail dock.
Quantified trust bar, AI fit-summary, reviews with breakdown + AI digest.
Sticky contact rail · full mobile parity.

02

Immersive

Penta design system, full-bleed · profile-immersive.html

The same product, turned into an experience. As you scroll, the portfolio images zoom and dive toward you, then resolve into the full profile — a landing-page-grade moment that still does the marketplace job.

Scroll-driven zoom-parallax hero gallery (a vanilla port of the Framer-Motion pattern).
Full-width, edge-to-edge — fixed to fill large screens.
Graceful fallback to a static grid on mobile / reduced-motion.

03

Editorial Noir

Custom design system · profile-noir.html

A complete reimagining — a cinematic, gallery-grade studio with its own dark, editorial design language. Doesn't load the Fiverr DS at all: serif display, a single warm-gold accent, film grain, and a horizontal pinned reel.

Horizontal pinned reel — scroll drives a film-strip of works with editorial captions.
Fraunces serif + Space Mono labels + gold accents + grain.
Still a real marketplace page — nav, trust, AI fit, hire flow all present.

Which one, when

Pick by the job, not the looks.

Ship the marketplace
→ Classic

Lowest risk, fully on-system, mobile-ready. The safe default that converts and reassures.

Make people stop
→ Immersive

For a hero seller, a campaign, or a "featured creator" spotlight where the wow earns the scroll.

Sell a premium brand
→ Editorial Noir

For top-tier creative sellers where the page itself is part of the portfolio. A distinct, ownable identity.
